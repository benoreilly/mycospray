Our Method

MycoSpray uses a highly effective electrostatic spray system to deliver sanitizers & disinfecting solutions to every surface for complete microscopic coverage of every unseen nook and cranny.

Maximal Coverage

Electrostatic sprayers expel charged droplets at a force 75 times stronger than the pull of Earth's gravity. This process ensures that the solution makes contact with all surface bacteria and viruses.

Environmentally Sound

Electrostatic sprayers reduce chemical waste, use low-toxicity chemicals, and lowers exposure to chemicals.
